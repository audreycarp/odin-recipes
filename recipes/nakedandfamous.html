Naked And Famous

About The Drink

It was invented in 2011 by bartender Joaquín Simó while working at the bar Death and Co in New York City. He is said to have claimed that this drink is the “bastard love child” of the Last Word and the Paper Plane.

I think that this is the perfect cocktail. It has a perfect balance of sweet, sour, smoke, and herbaciousness.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Mezcal
  • 1 oz Yellow Chartreuse
  • 1 oz Aperol
  • 1 oz Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a cocktail shaker
  2. Add enough ice to fill the shaker and shake vigorously
  3. Strain the cocktail into a coupe glass
  4. Garnish the drink with a lime wheel
