Paper Plane

About The Drink

Invented in 2002 by famous bartender Chad McDaniels at hip New York City bar Crust, this cocktail was an instant hit. It is considered a modern classic.

Its use of multiple amaros was revolutionary at the time. It has since become a staple at craft bars, and it has gone on to inspire a host of other bold cocktails.

Ingredients

  • .75 oz Bourbon
  • .75 oz Campari
  • .75 oz Amaro Nonino
  • .75 oz Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a cocktail shaker
  2. Add enough ice to fill the shaker and shake vigorously
  3. Strain the cocktail into a coupe glass
  4. Garnish the drink with a lemon twist
