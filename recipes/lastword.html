About The Drink

The original famous equal part cocktail. It originated in Scotland in 1933. It was passed down from king to king for 100 years before being published in a book in London.

Several of the other cocktails on this website were lovingly developed as odes to this drink. By keeping the main liquor as a compliment to the bold liquers this cocktail wows with its bold flavors.

Ingredients

  • .75 oz Gin
  • .75 oz Luxardo Cherry Liquer
  • .75 oz Green Chartreuse
  • .75 oz Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a cocktail shaker
  2. Add enough ice to fill the shaker and shake vigorously
  3. Strain the cocktail into a coupe glass
  4. Garnish the drink with a luxardo cherry or a lime wheel
